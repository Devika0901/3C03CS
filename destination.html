DESTINATION IN HIMACHAL

All Destinations

While traditionally Himachal was known as a summer destination, the Department of Tourism & Civil Aviation has taken special efforts to break the seasonality factor and has deceloped diversified tourism products to attract tourists in other seasons too. Now Himachal is known as "A DESTINATION FOR ALL SEASONS AND ALL REASONS". In fact, the Department has laid a speacial emphasis on the development of activity-based tourism and opening up of new sub destinaions. To promote tourism in the countryside and to unexplored areas,appropriate infrastructure is being developed wiithin available resources.
